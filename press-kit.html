Lock mark

The lock mark on its own (transparent background) — for use on dark backgrounds, social avatars, embeds.

  • PNG (586×586, transparent, white fill) — /press/586-lock-mark-white.png
  • SVG (vector, viewBox 0 0 586 586, single path, white fill) — /press/586-lock-mark.svg

The SVG version is preferred for any digital use — it stays sharp at any size, and the white fill can be re-colored in CSS via fill or color.

Usage guidelines

  • Don't alter the lock mark's proportions, stroke weights, or the chat-bubble keyhole.
  • Don't add effects (shadows, glows, gradients) that aren't in the original asset.
  • The white lock mark is intended for use over a contrasting (dark or colored) background. On light backgrounds, use the full app icon (which has its own blue background built in).
  • Brand colors: the icon's blue gradient is the primary brand color. The lock mark itself is white in product, but can be tinted to any accent color when used in-app.

About Enigmus

Enigmus runs large language models entirely on your device — no accounts, no servers, no telemetry, no network required after the initial model download. Built on Apple's MLX framework, the app uses on-device inference on Apple silicon (iPhone 13+, iPad with M1+, or any Mac with Apple silicon).
